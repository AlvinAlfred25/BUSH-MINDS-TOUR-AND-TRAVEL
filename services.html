Our Services

Our Safari Packages

Experiences Designed for You

Gorilla Trekking

The jewel of Uganda travel. Trek through Bwindi Impenetrable Forest or Mgahinga Gorilla National Park to spend an unforgettable hour with a wild mountain gorilla family. Our guides secure permits, prepare you for the trek, and ensure a respectful, awe-inspiring encounter.

  • Gorilla trekking permit assistance
  • Experienced wilderness guide
  • Transport to and from park
  • Accommodation options (budget to luxury)

Wildlife Safaris

Uganda's national parks are home to the Big Five and much more. From the tree-climbing lions of Ishasha to the giant herds at Murchison Falls, our game drives and boat safaris put you at the heart of Africa's wildlife spectacle.

  • Morning & evening game drives
  • Nile boat safaris
  • Bird watching with expert birding guides
  • Customisable multi-day packages

Adventure & Hiking Tours

For those who want to go deeper. Trek the Rwenzori Mountains, explore the Sipi Falls trail, or hike the volcanic peaks of Mgahinga. Adrenaline and altitude await the adventurous traveller.

  • Guided mountain treks
  • Waterfall hikes and abseiling
  • Camping arrangements
  • Safety equipment provided

Cultural & Community Tours

Africa's soul lives in its people. Visit the Batwa Pygmies of Bwindi, the Karamojong herders of the northeast, or enjoy a traditional Ugandan village stay. These experiences build bridges that last a lifetime.

  • Community visit facilitation
  • Cultural performance evenings
  • Local cuisine experiences
  • Craft market visits

Airport Transfers & Transport

Seamless arrivals and departures. We handle pick-ups and drop-offs from Entebbe International Airport, inter-city transfers, and 4WD hire for long-distance safari routes — so you never have to worry about getting around.

  • Entebbe Airport transfers
  • 4WD safari vehicle hire
  • City tours and day trips
  • Corporate transport solutions

Accommodation & Travel Planning

From budget-friendly safari lodges to exclusive tented camps, we handle all accommodation bookings. We also assist with Uganda visa information, travel insurance guidance, and itinerary planning from start to finish.

  • Lodge and camp bookings
  • Customised itinerary design
  • Visa information assistance
  • Group travel coordination
