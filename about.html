Who We Are

Born from the Bush

Bush Minds Tours & Travel is a Uganda-based tour and travel company rooted in the belief that the African bush is not just a place to visit — it is a place to belong. Founded and headquartered in Wakiso, just outside Kampala, we serve travellers from across Uganda and the world who want more than a packaged tour. They want an experience that feels alive.

Our name says it all. The "bush mind" is a way of seeing: patient, observant, attuned to nature's rhythms. It is the state of mind you fall into when a gorilla looks you in the eye, or when the Nile roars beneath Murchison Falls, or when the savannah stretches endlessly gold at dusk. We create the conditions for that state of mind to emerge.

Operating across Uganda's most magnificent national parks and beyond, Bush Minds specialises in gorilla trekking, wildlife safaris, cultural tours, and bespoke adventure packages for solo travellers, couples, families, and corporate groups.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted and immersive safari experience — where every journey deepens the traveller's connection to Africa's natural world.

Our Mission

To deliver extraordinary, safe, and personalised travel experiences across Uganda and East Africa — rooted in local knowledge, guided by passion, and measured by the smiles of our guests.

Our Values

Authenticity. Safety. Environmental respect. Community support. We believe that responsible tourism is the only tourism worth doing.
